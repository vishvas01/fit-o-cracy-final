Weight training

Type of strength training that uses weights for resistance. Weight training provides a stress to the muscles that causes them to adapt and get stronger, similar to the way aerobic conditioning strengthens your heart

More

cardio training

It is the type of training where your heart rate and breathing increase, but not so much that you feel like you need to stop and rest after a short period of time. Think running, speed walking, stair climbing, cycling, and swimming, among other activities

More

Yoga

It is type of training in which you move your body into various positions in order to become more fit or flexible, to improve your breathing, and to relax your mind

More
